## Feature Requests

### High Priority
1. **Opening Preparation**
   - Build opening repertoire
   - Track opening performance
   - Suggest improvements

2. **Game Review Mode**
   - Step through game with analysis
   - Annotated PGN export
   - Video-style playback

3. **Comparison Tools**
   - Compare with titled players
   - Compare with rating bracket
   - Progress tracking

### Medium Priority
1. **Tournament Analysis**
   - Aggregate tournament stats
   - Round-by-round performance
   - Opponent preparation

2. **Training Mode**
   - Find your mistakes
   - Practice critical positions
   - Timed challenges

3. **Social Features**
   - Follow friends
   - Share interesting games
   - Community discussions

### Low Priority
1. **Variants Support**
   - Chess960
   - Crazyhouse
   - Three-check
   - King of the Hill

2. **Historical Analysis**
   - Analyze classical games
   - Compare with grandmasters
   - Opening history
